Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Cake Bliss

What's included in the setup cost?

The ₹4,999 setup cost includes: system configuration for your bakery, data migration assistance (if needed), staff training sessions, WhatsApp Business API setup, and custom report configuration.

How do WhatsApp messages work?

We integrate with WhatsApp Business API to automatically send: order confirmations, delivery updates, birthday greetings, payment reminders, and promotional messages. You get 500 messages included monthly.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! There's no long-term contract. You can cancel your monthly subscription anytime. The setup fee is non-refundable after 30 days.

Do I need technical knowledge to use it?

Not at all! Cake Bliss is designed for bakery owners and staff. We provide complete training and the interface is intuitive. You'll be up and running in less than a day.

What about data security?

Your data is secured with enterprise-grade encryption, regular backups, and secure cloud hosting. We never share your customer data with third parties.
